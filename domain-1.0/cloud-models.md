

<p><h1>Cloud Deployment Models</h1></p>

<p><h3>Public Clouds</h3></p>

<p>Available to everyone over the internet</p>

<p><h3>Private Clouds</h3></p>

<p>Your own localized data center</p>

<p><h3>Hybrid Cloud</h3></p>

<p>A combination of public facing and private facing systems.</p>

<p><h1>Software as a Service (SaaS)</h1></p>

<p>On-demand software, not locally installed, centralized management of data, a complete offering. (i.e M365/Google Suite)</p>

<p><h1>Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)</h1></p>

<p>Sometimes called HaaS (Hardware as a Service) means that the phyiscal hardware is not hosted on-site.</p>

<p><h1>Platform as a Service (PaaS)</h1></p>

<p>No servers, no software, no maintenance team, someone handles the platform, you handle the dev.</p>

**<p>Note you don't have direct control of the data, people, or infrastructure</p>**
