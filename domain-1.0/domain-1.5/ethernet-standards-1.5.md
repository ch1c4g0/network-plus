<p><h1>Deciphering the Standard</h1></p>

<p>Speed, signal, and media all of these are contained within the name.</p>

<p><h3>Numerics</h3></p>

<p>The number is related to the network speed, 1000 is commonly 1,000 megabits per second/ one gigabit/sec. 10G would be 10 gigabits per seconds.</p>

<p><h3>BASE(Baseband)</h3></p>

<p>single frequency using the entire medium, while broadband uses many frequencies sharing the medium.</p>

<p><h3>Media Type</h3></p>

<p>T is twisted pair copper, F is fiber, SX is short wavelength light</p>
