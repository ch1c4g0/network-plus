

<p><h1>SC - Subscriber Connector</h1></p>

<p>Has been called square connector, standard connector, or it's actual name square connector.<br>
<p>These connectors push into place and lock, if you pull on the connector it will unlock.<br>
Commonly in pairs of transmit - receive.</p>

<p><h1>LC - Local Connector</h1></p>

<p>Locks in place with a clip, also called Lucent or Little Connectors.<br>
They can also be combined together, one for transmit, one for receive.</p>

<p><h1>ST - Straight Tip</h1></p>

<p>Uses a bayontet connector (stick and twist) (push on and turn)</p>

<p><h1>MPO - Multi-Fiber Push On</h1></p>

<p>Allows for 12 fibers in a single connector, it locks similar to the SC connector push to lock pull to unlock.<br>
Also commonly abbreviated as MTP or MTP MPO connector</p>
